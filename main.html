A flower, sometimes known as a bloom or blossom, is the reproductive structure found in flowering plants (plants of the division Angiospermae). Flowers produce gametophytes, which in flowering plants consist of a few haploid cells which produce gametes.

Rose is a shrub. Roses are of many colours. Rose is considered as a symbol of balance. Red rose is an expression of love while a deep red rose can be used to express regret and sorrow. The name Rose is derived from Latin word Rosa. Rose belong to the family of plants called Rosaceae.
